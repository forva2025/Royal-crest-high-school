About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1985, Royal Crest High School has grown from humble beginnings to become one of Uganda's premier educational institutions. What started as a small community school with just 50 students has now flourished into a center of academic excellence with over 1,200 students.

Our commitment to holistic education has remained unchanged through the decades, producing leaders who have made significant contributions to Uganda and beyond in various fields including medicine, law, engineering, and the arts.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and morally, preparing them to be responsible citizens and leaders.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, recognized nationally and internationally for developing well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, and Community. These core values guide our daily interactions and decision-making processes.

School Timeline

1985 - Foundation

Royal Crest High School was established with two classrooms and three teachers.

1992 - First Expansion

Added science laboratories and expanded to accommodate 200 students.

2001 - A-Level Introduced

Began offering Advanced Level education with three combinations.

2010 - National Recognition

Ranked among top 10 schools in Uganda for academic performance.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning infrastructure and e-learning platform.

Step 1: Inquiry

Begin your journey with Royal Crest High School by making an inquiry. You can:

  • Visit our campus during visiting hours
  • Call our admissions office at +256 700 000000
  • Email us at admissions@royalcrest.ac.ug
  • Fill out the inquiry form on our website

Next Step: Complete the application process

Step 2: Application

Submit your application with the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school report cards
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photos (2 copies)
  • Medical form completed by a licensed physician

Next Step: Wait for shortlisting results

Step 3: Shortlist Successful Candidates

Successful candidates will be shortlisted based on their application materials and academic records. The shortlisted candidates will be notified through their provided contact information.

Next Step: Final admission offers will be made to the shortlisted candidates

Step 4: Admission

Upon receiving an admission offer, you will need to:

  • Accept the admission offer within the specified timeframe
  • Pay the required admission fees
  • Complete the registration process
  • Attend the orientation program

Requirements

O-Level (S1-S4)

  • Completed P7 with good grades
  • Pass in English and Mathematics
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report cards
  • Passport photos (2 copies)

A-Level (S5-S6)

  • UCE certificate with at least 8 passes
  • Minimum of credit 5 in relevant subjects
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report cards
  • Passport photos (2 copies)
